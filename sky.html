Sky

What is Sky?

"Sky" is the region of the Earth's atmosphere that is visible from the surface during the day. It appears as a dome or an expanse that can range from blue to various shades during the day and turns dark during the night.

Colours of Sky

  • Blue Sky
  • Sunrise and Sunset Colors
  • Twilight Colors
  • Gray or Overcast Sky
  • Night Sky
  • Stormy Sky
